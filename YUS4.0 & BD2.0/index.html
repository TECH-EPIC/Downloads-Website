YUS 4.0 & BD 2.0 for openBVE

The Yonge-University-Spadina (YUS) line V4.0 and Bloor-Danforth (BD) line V2.0 for OpenBVE.

YUS 4.0 goes from Finch to Vaughan Metropolitan Centre and BD 2.0 goes from Kennedy to Kipling.
YUS 4.0 does have a Vaughn Metropolitan Centre to Finch route, but it is not included in the installer.
Stay tuned for when that route is released.
BD 2.0 does not have a Kipling to Kennedy route.

Whats Included:
  • Improved sounds, physics for all trains and updated panel for H5/H6
  • Improved station textures and objects
  • Improved grade time signals
  • Improved outdoor scenery
  • Remastered H6 with improved interior, retirement livery, subway operator, black light outline and classic seats skin
  • Added animations including vehicles and other trains
  • Realistic track flange sounds for certain sections
  • New Toronto Rocket announcements
  • Rain and snow versions
All downloads Require OpenAL!
