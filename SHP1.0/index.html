YUS 3.6 & BD 1.2 & SHP 1.0 for openBVE

The Yonge-University-Spadina (YUS) line V3.6, Bloor-Danforth (BD) line V1.2 and Sheppard (SHP) line V1.0 for OpenBVE.

YUS 3.6 and BD 1.2 are the previous and og versions of the Toronto subway routes for openBVE.
With the addition of the Sheppard line, SHP 1.0, this was what the TTC Academy used for all their runs.
YUS 3.6 and BD 1.2 do not feature in-game signals, but SHP 1.0 does.

All downloads Require OpenAL!
